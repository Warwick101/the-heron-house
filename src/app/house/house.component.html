The house was built by a local builder in Hermanus at the time. I guess just after the war. His name wan Jack Moore He built the house for himself to live in. Oupa bought it about 1961 I guess.At the time 9th ave was a gravel road. There was no street behind. The milkwood trees on Heron House and the ones on Milkwood lodge behind formed one big milkwood forest with the little river running next to it There were only about three or four houses round us One of them was Mossienes, Charlyn's thatched roof house It then belonged to Charlyn s husband s father and her husband was then a kid like us but we never really played together I don't know why The property and the milkwood forest was certainly spoilt by the new busy road Oupa modified the house in about 1976 and moved to live there permanently in about 1978
